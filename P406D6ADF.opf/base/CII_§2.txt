His countless riches saved and embraced the poor.By means of the skillful means, he embraced
all immoral beings;With the practice of patience and self-control, he embraced all living
beings who had hatred and anger.[Even though] he was a layman, he made earnest efforts to
embrace people who were lazy.With meditation and full concentration, he embraced living beings
who had deluded minds.He attained the certainty of wisdom for the sake of embracing
all who had wrong knowledge.
